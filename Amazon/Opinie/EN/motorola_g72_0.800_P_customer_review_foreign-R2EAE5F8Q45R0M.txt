This Motorola Moto g72 smartphone (6.6" FHD+ display, 108 MP camera, 6/128 GB, 5000 mAh fulfills most of the promises, but the camera with a high-resolution chip doesn't seem to be that high-resolution, if you zoom in after taking the picture, coarse pixels appear almost immediately. Face recognition is like the sensor for fingerprint recognition, sometimes it works and several times it doesn't... But as a second device for abroad, a good choice and Motorola Moto g72 can definitely convince you.
